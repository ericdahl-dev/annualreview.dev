Terms

AnnualReview.dev is provided as a drafting tool for evidence-backed annual review material. You are responsible for verifying the final content before sharing it with a manager, committee, or employer.

Use

  • Use the app only with data that you are authorized to access.
  • Review generated text before treating it as factual.
  • Do not rely on the app to confirm private employment policy.

Limitations

  • The app may label uncertain impact as needing confirmation.
  • Generated output depends on the quality of the evidence provided.
  • Self-hosted deployments carry their own operational and privacy risks.
